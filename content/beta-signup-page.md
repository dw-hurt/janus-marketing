## Frequently Asked Questions

### About the Beta

**Q: Is there a cost to join the beta?**  
A: Yes, but at 50% off regular pricing (announced at launch). Our goal is to charge less than one month of a typical parental control subscription for the entire year.

**Q: What if I don't like it?**  
A: 30-day money-back guarantee. No questions asked.

**Q: How many devices can I use?**  
A: Beta pricing includes up to 3 child devices. Need more? Contact us for family plans.

**Q: Will my data be safe?**  
A: Absolutely. We're COPPA, GDPR-K, and PIPEDA compliant from day one. Your data is encrypted and never sold.

**Q: What happens after beta ends?**  
A: You keep using Janus at your beta discount rate. Forever.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which Android versions are supported?**  
A: Android 8.0 (Oreo) or newer. Most phones from 2017+ work.

**Q: Do I need a cell phone plan?**  
A: No! WiFi-only works perfectly. That's the point.

**Q: Can I use a tablet instead of a phone?**  
A: Yes! Any Android tablet version 8+ works.

**Q: What about iOS/iPhone?**  
A: Not in beta. We're starting with Android. iOS support may come later based on demand.

**Q: Do I need to root or jailbreak the device?**  
A: Absolutely not. No rooting required. Standard installation.

### About Signing Up

**Q: When will I get access?**  
A: Beta invitations go out in June 2026 to first 500 signups (in order).

**Q: What if I don't have old phones yet?**  
A: Join the waitlist anyway! You have until June 2026 to find devices. Check drawers, ask family, or buy used (\-50 on eBay).

**Q: Can I sign up if I'm not in the US?**  
A: Yes! We support US, Canada, UK, EU, Australia, and most other countries.

**Q: Will there be a waitlist after 500 spots fill?**  
A: Yes. If beta fills up, you'll be first in line for public launch (September 2026).
